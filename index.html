About Us

Track Electronic Sports is a pioneering Bangladeshi esports community dedicated to empowering the next generation of competitive gamers. We are more than just a gaming platform — we are a movement built by gamers, for gamers. Our mission is to shape the future of esports in Bangladesh by creating a structured, inclusive, and thriving environment for players, teams, and organizers. From hosting thrilling online and offline leagues to assisting in the formation of new esports teams, Track Electronic Sports serves as a central hub for competitive gaming growth. We also specialize in the management and development of esports teams, offering support in team organization, branding, tournament preparation, and long-term strategy. Whether you're a solo player looking for a team, a group ready to take the next step, or an organizer wanting to host a tournament — Track Electronic Sports is here to guide and grow with you. Join the movement. Compete, connect, and create your legacy in Bangladesh’s booming esports scene.
